What Replyful does

Everything your inbox
needs to run itself

Works for any UK small business that receives customer enquiries by email.

⚡

Instant AI Replies

Every enquiry gets a genuinely helpful, personalised reply within seconds — written by AI in your voice, using your real pricing and services.

New
📅

Calendar Integration

Replyful checks your Google Calendar in real time. When a booking request arrives, it suggests your actual free slots — not a vague "we'll be in touch".

💬

Quote Approval

When a quote request comes in, Replyful drafts a full quote using your pricing and holds it for your approval. Nothing sends without your say-so.

🔁

Follow-Up Sequences

Unanswered quotes and bookings get a polite automatic chase after a set number of days. If the customer replies first, the follow-up cancels itself.

⏱

Human-like Timing

Set replies to arrive after a natural-feeling delay — 2 minutes, 20 minutes, or a few hours. Customers can't tell it's automated. Pro customers can enable instant replies.

🗂

Smart Email Triage

Every email is categorised and colour-coded — quotes, bookings, complaints, follow-ups — so you can see at a glance what genuinely needs your attention.

📊

Dashboard & Reports

A clear dashboard shows everything Replyful has handled — AI summaries of what customers are asking, pending bookings, draft quotes, and weekly reports.

🛡

Spam Filtering

Replyful automatically ignores automated emails, marketing messages, delivery notifications and no-reply senders — so you only pay for real customer replies.

How it works

Up and running
in minutes

01

Sign up and fill in your details

Tell Replyful about your business — your services, pricing, working hours and the tone you want. Takes about 5 minutes. Fully self-serve, no calls required.

02

Connect your inbox

Link your Gmail or Google Workspace account with one click. Optionally connect Google Calendar so Replyful can suggest real available times when booking requests arrive.

03

Your inbox runs itself

Every new enquiry gets a reply within seconds. Quotes are drafted for your approval. Bookings are confirmed. Follow-ups are chased. You just check in when you want to.

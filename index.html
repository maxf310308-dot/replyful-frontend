What Replyful does

Stop losing jobs
to a slow inbox

Works with Gmail and Google Workspace for any UK small business.

AI Replies

Every enquiry gets a helpful, personalised reply in seconds — written in your voice using your real pricing and services.

New

Calendar Integration

Your Google Calendar is checked in real time. Booking replies include your actual free slots — not a vague "someone will be in touch".

Quote Approval

Replyful drafts a full quote when one is requested. It waits for your approval — nothing sends without your say-so.

Follow-Up Sequences

Unanswered quotes and bookings get a polite chase after a set number of days. Cancels automatically if the customer replies.

Human-like Timing

Set replies to arrive after a natural delay — 4 minutes, 20 minutes, or a few hours. Customers respond better when it doesn't feel instant.

Smart Triage

Every email is categorised and colour-coded in your inbox — quotes, bookings, complaints, follow-ups — so you see what needs you at a glance.

Dashboard & Reports

See everything Replyful has handled — AI summaries, pending bookings, draft quotes, and a weekly report of time saved.

Spam Filtering

Automated emails, marketing messages and delivery notifications are ignored automatically — you only pay for real customer replies.
